Key Features & Components

Everything you need to discover, join, and manage student projects

A

Student Profiles

Showcase your interests, skills, and what you're working on or looking for.

  • Name, major, interests
  • Classes taken (optional)
  • Portfolio, GitHub, LinkedIn links
  • "Looking for / Working on" toggle
Why Admin Care
Normalizes projects for non-elite students, makes invisible learning visible, and supports equity reporting.
B

Project Pages

The heart of the platform‚Äîeach project tells its story in plain language.

  • Plain-language description
  • Skills used & skills learned
  • Status: Idea, In Progress, Recruiting, Archived
  • Learning community tags (MESA, Umoja, Puente)
Why Admin Care
Solves the continuity/brain drain problem and creates institutional memory without heavy staff lift.
C

Discovery & Tagging

Find exactly what you're looking for with smart filters and tags.

  • Tags: CS, Engineering, Social Justice, Business, Art
  • Filters: "Looking for collaborators," "Beginner-friendly"
  • Optional class filter (CS 1A, etc.)
Why Admin Care
Aligns with transfer/employer language, encourages interdisciplinary work, and reduces intimidation for first-gen students.
D

Join / Start Pathway

Lower the activation energy with guided prompts that help you begin.

  • "I don't know where to start"
  • "I have an idea but no team"
  • "I want experience but no internship"
Why Admin Care
Addresses the "I don't belong here" gap and supports international & Dream Act students.
E

Continuity & Maintenance

Keep projects alive and prevent abandonment with smart auto-checks.

  • "Is this project still active?" ping every 30-60 days
  • Archive instead of delete
  • Clear ownership (lead + backup)
Why Admin Care
Prevents ghost projects, lowers reputational risk, and makes the platform sustainable beyond current students.
F

Resource Integration

Connect to campus programs and increase the ROI of existing resources.

  • ETI, KCI, Innovation Challenge links
  • Startup Club integration
  • "Related resources" per project
Why Admin Care
Increases ROI of existing programs, reduces "too many websites" problem, and shows institutional alignment.
